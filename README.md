
# E-Commerce Revenue Management Analysis Using Python

## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce transaction data to evaluate company revenue, brand revenue, product performance, and regional sales trends. The objective is to derive actionable business insights that help improve profitability, optimize pricing strategies, and support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Objective

As a Business Analyst for an E-Commerce company, the goal was to:

* Analyze order and pricing data.
* Calculate company revenue and brand revenue.
* Identify top-performing brands.
* Understand regional and monthly sales performance.
* Generate insights to improve business growth and profitability.

## Dataset

The dataset contains e-commerce order information including:

* Product Category
* Brand
* Retail Price
* Discounted Price
* Product Ratings
* Order Date
* Region
* Revenue Metrics

## Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas

## Key Analysis Performed

### Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Loaded and explored the dataset.
* Identified data types and missing values.
* Removed non-informative columns.
* Prepared data for analysis.

### Revenue Analysis

* Calculated Net Revenue generated by the E-Commerce platform.
* Evaluated the impact of discounts and commissions on profitability.
* Analyzed revenue contribution across products and brands.

### Brand Performance Analysis

* Calculated Net Brand Revenue.
* Identified top-performing brands based on revenue.
* Compared brand-level sales and profitability.

### Product & Category Insights

* Examined product pricing trends.
* Analyzed category-wise sales performance.
* Evaluated customer ratings and product popularity.

### Regional Performance Analysis

* Compared monthly revenue across regions.
* Identified best and worst-performing months.
* Visualized regional revenue trends.

## Visualizations

The project includes visualizations such as:

* Revenue trend analysis
* Brand performance comparison
* Regional revenue line plots
* Pair plots for brand-level metrics
* Distribution and correlation analysis

## Key Business Insights

* Revenue generation varies significantly across brands and product categories.
* A small number of brands contribute a large share of total revenue.
* Regional sales patterns reveal seasonal business opportunities.
* Pricing and discount strategies directly impact profitability.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase focus on high-performing brands.
* Optimize discount strategies to improve net revenue.
* Strengthen marketing efforts in underperforming regions.
* Use seasonal trends for inventory and campaign planning.
